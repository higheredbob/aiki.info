ushiro-katate-eri-mochi sankajo-osae (2)
rear-one-hand-neck-grasp third-control-pin (2)

  1. ai-hanmi, uke attack with ushiro-katate-eri-mochi and push
  2. slide forward
  3. raise hands
  4. reverse pivot right over 180°, right hand take uke's hand
  5. reverse pivot right 135° to 180°, control with sankajo
  6. step back, bearing right, atemi to uke's face
  7. slide back, bearing left, left hand reverse grip uke's elbow
  8. step back and turn left 90° to 135°, drop to one knee
  9. pivot right 90° on the right knee
  10. lock with sankajo-osae
