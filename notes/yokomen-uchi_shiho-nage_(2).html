yokomen-uchi shiho-nage (2)
side-strike, four-direction throw (2)

  1. gyaku-hanmi, uke attacks with yokomen-uchi
  2. block, metsubushi atemi to uke's face, bear left, slide forward, lower the body
  3. pivot right 180°, left hand take uke's wrist
  4. turn right 180°, raise the body, lock with shiho-nage
  5. slide forward and throw uke, drop to one knee
  6. lock with shiho-nage
  7. tegatana atemi to uke's face
