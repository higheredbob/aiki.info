ryote-mochi shiho-nage (2)
two-hand grasp, four-direction throw (2)

  1. gyaku-hanmi, uke attack with ryote-mochi and push
  2. pivot right 180°, lower the body, left hand take uke's wrist
  3. turn right 180°, raise the body, control with shiho-nage
  4. slide forward and throw uke, drop to one knee
  5. lock with shiho-nage
  6. tegatana atemi to uke's face
