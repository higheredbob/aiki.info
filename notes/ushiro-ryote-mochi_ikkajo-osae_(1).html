  2. reverse pivot right 45°
  3. raise hands
  4. step back and pivot right 45°, lower the body

  6. slide forward, control with ikkajo
  7. step forward, bearing right
  8. step forward, drop to one knee
  9. drop to kiza, bearing right
  10. lock with ikkajo-osae
