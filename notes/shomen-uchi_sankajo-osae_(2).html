shomen-uchi sankajo-osae (2)
front-strike third-control-pin (2)

  1. gyaku-hanmi, uke attack with shomen-uchi
  2. right hand block, left hand take uke's elbow
  3. pivot right 135° to 180°, right hand take uke's hand
  4. slide left and forward to face uke, left hand take uke's wrist
  5. reverse pivot left 135°, control with sankajo
  6. step back, bearing left, lower the body, atemi to uke's face
  7. slide back, bearing right, right hand take uke's elbow (reverse grip)
  8. step back and turn right 90°, drop to one knee
  9. pivot left 90° on the left knee
  10. lock with sankajo-osae
