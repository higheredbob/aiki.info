yokomen-uchi shiho-nage (1)
side-strike, four-direction throw (1)

  1. ai-hanmi, uke attack with yokomen-uchi
  2. left hand block, metsubushi atemi, pivot left 135° to 180°
  3. bear right, slide forward, lower the body, left hand take uke's wrist
  4. step forward, bearing left
  5. turn right 180°, raise the body, control with shiho-nage
  6. slide forward and throw uke, drop to one knee
  7. lock with shiho-nage
  8. tegatana atemi to uke's face
