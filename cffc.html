Morning meets normally start at 6:30am or so, we can vary it.
Evening meets usually start at 7pm or so, we can vary it.
Daytime meets could be any time during the day, depending when people are available.

We normally meet outside in one of several local parks in the Coburg / Fawkner area,
such as Mutton Reserve, Bridges Reserve or Coburg Lake Reserve for example.

NB: The meets are not violent or dangerous!
